helping workers collect what they deserve

Hello, we are workerbird. We help workers understand and improve their working conditions.


With workerbird, workers collect their own data, on their own terms and for their own benefit. They receive advice and support if needed.


We want workers to realise the power in their data.

features

Track and understand your working life

We've developed easy to use, worker-centred tracking.  This enables workers to easily track their shifts, travel time, breaks and record pay.

Track and understand your working life

Get insights into your working day

We provide insights and information about the tracked hours and pay. This helps workers understand their working conditions in a broader context.

Get insights into your working day

Access advice and support

Where needed we provide the worker with relevant information on next steps, such as reporting or where to access further help.
