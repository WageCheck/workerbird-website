get involved

workerbird is looking for testers for its first version.


Track your working hours, commute, work travel and unpaid/paid breaks using a timer or manually. Get visual reports on your working day, weekly hours and your pay over time. Understand whether your hourly rate is above the national minimum wage and how it compares to the real living wage.

contact us

As we develop beyond the first version of workerbird, we would like to hear from interested organisations and individuals so please get in touch.


We are looking for partner organisations to develop and trial specific features with us. These could be specific to a sector (such as recording tips in hospitality) or relevant to all workers (such as holiday and sick pay). We look forward to hearing from you.
